Assam

List of Major Projects

  • Highway and bridge projects to improve connectivity in the Brahmaputra valley.
  • Flood and erosion control works along major river stretches.
  • Upgradation of key railway stations and routes for better passenger facilities.

Ongoing Developments

  • Improving healthcare and education facilities in districts and rural areas.
  • Tourism development around national parks and river-island destinations.
  • Digital services and public-service delivery improvements through e-governance.

Importance of the State

  • Gateway state to the North-East region of India.
  • Major producer of tea and has natural resources like oil and gas.
  • Known for biodiversity and strong cultural heritage.

Tourist Attractions

  • Kaziranga National Park
  • Manas National Park
  • Majuli River Island
  • Kamakhya Temple, Guwahati
  • Sivasagar (Ahom heritage sites)
